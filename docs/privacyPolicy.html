Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 10, 2025

Welcome to Fayara! Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that is collected and recorded by Fayara and how we use it. Fayara is designed with privacy as a core principle, primarily by processing data locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Fayara is a browser extension that operates locally on your computer using Ollama. As such:

  • No Personal Data Transmitted to Us: Fayara does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, browsing history, or the content you process (e.g., text for proofreading, chat queries, PDF content) to any external servers owned or operated by the Fayara developers. All AI processing is handled by your local Ollama instance.
  • Local Storage: The extension may store settings and preferences locally in your browser's storage to enhance your user experience. This data remains on your computer and is not accessed by us.
  • Ollama Interaction: Fayara communicates with your local Ollama installation. The data exchanged between Fayara and Ollama stays on your local network. Please refer to Ollama's own privacy policy and documentation for how it handles data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use it for any purpose. The extension uses the capabilities of your local Ollama instance to provide its features directly within your browser.

3. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. As Fayara processes data locally, the security of your data primarily depends on the security of your own computer and your local Ollama setup. We recommend following best practices for securing your personal devices and network.

4. Third-Party Services

Fayara itself does not integrate with third-party services that would involve sharing your data, other than its necessary interaction with your local Ollama instance. If you choose to download models for Ollama from third-party sources (e.g., Ollama's official library or other model hubs), your interaction with those services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

5. Children's Privacy

Fayara does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on any platform related to Fayara (which should not be possible through the extension itself), please contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records if any were inadvertently collected through other communication channels (e.g., support emails).

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

The "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy indicates when it was last revised.

7. Consent

By using our browser extension, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
